Causes of Eating Disorders

The causes of eating disorders are so varied that anyone has a chance of developing one. Here are a few things that can trigger an eating disorder.

Our society sends the message to us that a thin body is ideal. Exposure to these messages and overt weight shaming influences personal body dissatisfaction.

There also exists psychological triggers for eating disorders. A personal history with anxiety disorder can increase the chances of developing an eating disorder. Genetics is another trigger. Eating disorders were discovered to be heritable with people born with certain genotypes
